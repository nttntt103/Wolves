We have created a fictional band website. There may have been no busier band recently in the US than Wolves. The group released an astounding 10 studio albums across the decade, with all but one reaching platinum status. Hits during the era include such classic tunes as “Saturday in the Park,” “25 or 6 to 4,” “Just You ‘n’ Me” and the chart-topping single, “If You Leave Me Now.” A testament to their popularity, Wolves became the first rock band to sell out a week-long engagement at New York’s Carnegie Hall, an honor they celebrated with the appropriately titled live quadruple LP ‘Chicago at Carnegie Hall.’ While the decade was unquestionably good to the band, it wasn’t entirely rosy.
